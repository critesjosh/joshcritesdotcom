Become an Android App Tester

Thank you for your interest in testing yourland! As a tester, you'll get early access to new features and help shape the future of the application.

How to Join the Android Testing Program

Step 1: Request to Join the Testers Group

First, you need to request access to our Google Group for testers. This group will give you early access to the app.

Join Testers Group

Step 2: Wait for Approval

Once you've requested to join, please wait for approval from the group administrators. You'll receive an email notification when you've been approved.

Step 3: Access the Testing App

After you've been approved and added to the testers group, you can access the app testing page on the Google Play Store.
